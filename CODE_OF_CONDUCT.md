# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in the DeskGraph community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of background, identity, experience level, or personal characteristics.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, constructive, and specific.
- Critique ideas and evidence, not people.
- Protect user privacy and avoid sharing private files or paths.
- Disclose limitations and security concerns honestly.
- Welcome contributors who are learning.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, threats, discriminatory language, unwanted sexual attention, deliberate exposure of private information, sustained disruption, and retaliation are not tolerated.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns through the repository's private maintainer contact once it is published. Until then, ask the repository owner to establish a private contact channel. Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and participation that violate this policy.

## Attribution

This policy is inspired by the Contributor Covenant, version 2.1, available at https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct.html.
