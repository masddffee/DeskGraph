| R-002 | Data loss or unrecoverable move/rename                                      | Medium     | High   | ADR-017 preview plus ADR-025's bounded SHA-256/root/parent/source binding, immutable requests, closed append-only states, lease and observation-only recovery protocol pass deterministic tests. Review found two unsafe assumptions: an expiring lease cannot fence a stopped live executor, and Unix leaf-name rename can move a replacement ordinary file after the final identity check. Existing previews also lack the future immutable original-destination/receipt companion and are permanently non-confirmable. Future confirmation must rerun equivalent live validation and invalidate any changed candidate/keeper/evidence/source rather than trust the historical Preview. Trash is an action-bound capability and cannot become a scope or be enumerated/searched/exposed. Rules, notifications, LLMs, batches and MCP cannot bypass confirmation. Batches are capped at 100 items/100 GiB, stop on the first non-completed item and preserve per-item outcomes/Undo without batch rollback. Permanent delete and empty-trash capabilities do not exist.
